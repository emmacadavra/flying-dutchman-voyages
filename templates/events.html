Doomed Easter Sunday Voyage...

Sail with us 'til Doomsday!

It was on that fateful day in 1641 on which Captain Van der Decken ordered that The Flying Dutchman set sail from the Cape of Good Hope, against the superstitious wishes of his crew - proclaiming loudly that he would round the Cape, even if he had to sail until Doomsday!
